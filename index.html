PhD Student in Economics, Harvard University

Welcome!

I am a PhD student in Economics at Harvard University. I am interested in Development Economics, Economic History, Cultural Economics, and Political Economy.

My work studies how historical institutions and cultural factors shape long-run development, combining archival data construction with applied microeconometric methods. I graduated from Universidad de los Andes with a BA in Economics (2021), and an MA in Economics (2023). I'm originally from Bogotá, Colombia !
