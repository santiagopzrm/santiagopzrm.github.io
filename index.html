I am a PhD student in Economics at Harvard University. I am interested in Development Economics, Economic History, Cultural Economics, and Political Economy.

My work studies how historical institutions and cultural factors shape long-run development, combining archival data construction with applied microeconometric methods. I'm originally from Bogotá, Colombia!
